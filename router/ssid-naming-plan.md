# SSID Naming Plan

## Summary
This document tracks the naming approach for future SSIDs in the home lab.

## Current Goal
Use SSID names that do not expose unnecessary router or hardware information.

## Naming Principles
- avoid vendor or model-identifying names
- use clear but neutral labels
- keep names easy to recognize for management purposes
- align names with the future network design

## Current Status
SSID naming has been planned at a basic level, but final implementation is being delayed until the switch and firewall are fully integrated.

## Reason for Delay
Final SSID structure should align with:
- segmentation goals
- active topology
- firewall policy direction
- long-term network organization
